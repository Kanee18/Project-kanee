FAQ

What is Kanee, exactly?

A 3D anime AI companion that runs in your browser. You talk to her by voice or text and she replies out loud while her avatar animates in real time.

How do I get in during the beta?

Create an account, then contact us to request access. Once you're approved, the "Start chat with Kanee" button unlocks on your account.

Does it cost anything right now?

No — the beta is completely free. Paid monthly plans only go live at launch, and beta testers will get a heads-up first.

Do I need a powerful PC?

The avatar renders in your browser, so a reasonably modern machine with a decent GPU gives the smoothest experience. It still works without one, just less buttery.

Is my data private?

Your account is managed through Firebase. Conversations power Kanee's memory so she can remember you between chats. We don't sell your data.

Want in on the beta?

Kanee is in private testing while we polish her. Make an account, then reach out and we'll grant your access by hand.
